## Version: Beta
## Date: 12/29
 
### Functionalities: 
- Webscrape data from the Pokémon TCGP archives
- Accepts an input list of Pokémon to find their associated packs
- Recommends the best pack containing the most input Pokémon

### Changes:

### Known Bugs:
- pack_rec does not take into account rarity or odds of finding the given pokemon in the pack
- pack_rec may fail to webscrape data for unusual table setups

### Future Plans:
- Incorporate card odds
- Incorporate additional information, like pack points, which could influence the pack recommendation
- Provide independent meta relevant recommendations for users who would like to build relevant decks
